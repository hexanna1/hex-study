About This Study

These numbers are local move stone percents for the side to move. 100 means the strongest local reply, and 0 is the pass baseline.

They are computed by pooling equivalent local replies across symmetry-related balanced positions and subtracting a matched baseline to reduce edge and whole-board effects. A typical local move is pooled over 24 KataHex raw-NN evaluations (12 D6 symmetries × 2 for the matched ablation baseline).
